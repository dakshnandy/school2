Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978.

Welcome to our school church, a sanctuary where students find solace, inspiration, and community. Nestled at the heart of our campus, the school church serves as a place for reflection and spiritual growth. Here, students gather for meaningful worship services, engaging discussions, and moments of quiet contemplation.



Library

Was built in 1980.

The school library, a sanctuary of knowledge and imagination, stands as a quiet refuge amidst the bustling corridors and classrooms. Rows of shelves laden with books of every genre and era beckon students to explore realms both familiar and fantastical. Here, whispers of turning pages mingle with the soft rustle of curiosity, as minds young and eager delve into worlds crafted by words.



Auditorium

Was built in 1982.

The school auditorium, a grand stage awaiting its performers, echoes with the anticipation of creativity and expression. Bathed in the soft glow of stage lights, its spacious confines accommodate the collective heartbeat of students and faculty alike. Here, the magic of theater unfolds, where rehearsals transform into captivating performances that captivate audiences.
